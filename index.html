The arcadia projet

Goal

Reunion has a very high rate of endemic plants. The arcadia project (the name may change) aims to build a park with all endemic trees in Reunion Island. For every tree, a sign with an explanation will be installed. I plan to open to public. The goal is to make children learn about importance of nature preservation. 10% of the money will go to an association named "Zarlor". They want to plant trees in front of building in underprivileged areas
